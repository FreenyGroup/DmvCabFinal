    What we do

    We specialize in End-to-End supply chain services, sourcing/procurement, logistics, shipping, relocation,
    warehousing, customs clearence, packing, diplomatic cargoes, personal effects; Including import and export.

    Shipping & Freight Forwarding

    • Air, Ocean, and Land, Freight Forwarding
    • All export, import, cross stuffing and cross trade shipments.
    • International and local land transport logistics solutions
    • FCL & LCL consignment around the world
    • Container transportation, Land transporation, LTL & FTL

    Warehousing

    • Strategically located warehouses across multiple locations around the world
    • Easy access to all major sea, air, & road networks.
    • Space available at both free zone and local warehouses
    • Equipment available on-site for off-loading and on-loading cargo
    • Temperature controlled warehouse, Labeling, pick & pack.

    Cargo Handling & packaging

    • We are licensed clearence agency
    • Authorized for cargo clearence and forwarding globally
    • Experienced ground staff for cargo handling process
    • Experienced professionals for unpacking and repacking
    • Serving in all the major countries

    Customs clearence

    • Custom clearing at all commercial ports of entry in the UAE
    • All export/import & transit clearences by Sea, Air, and Land.
    • Immediate processing of custom clearing with all documentation
    • Dedicated staff for coordination and updates
    • Best custom clearing rates guarantee
